Grade-Level Focus

Personalized support for rising 6th–9th grade students

Rising 6th Grade

Strengthen foundations and build confidence for middle school math.

  • Multiplication and division
  • Fractions and decimals
  • Ratios and patterns
  • Multi-step word problems
  • Number sense
  • Showing work clearly

Rising 7th Grade

Build fluency and prepare for more advanced concepts.

  • Fractions, decimals, and percents
  • Ratios and proportional relationships
  • Integer operations
  • One- and two-step equations

Rising 8th Grade

Strengthen pre-Algebra skills and prepare for higher math.

  • Solving equations
  • Slope and graphing

  • Algebra readiness

Rising 9th Grade

Build Algebra foundations and start high school ready to succeed.

  • Multi-step equations
  • Variables on both sides
  • Linear functions
  • Slope and y-intercept
  • Graphing lines
  • Systems of equations

How it works

Simple, structured, and online

  2. We choose the highest-impact focus areas.
  3. Your student attends four 60-minute online sessions.
  4. You receive a quick parent update after each session.
