Back-to-School Math Reset Website

Files included:
- index.html
- styles.css

How to use:
1. Open index.html in a browser to preview the site.
2. Replace the placeholder contact line with your real contact method or booking link.
3. Replace the mailto link near the bottom with your email address.
4. Add your tutor photo in Canva or by editing the photo placeholder section in index.html/CSS.
5. Upload both index.html and styles.css to any static website host such as Netlify, GitHub Pages, Carrd custom embed, or your own hosting.
